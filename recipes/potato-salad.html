Potato Salad

Description

There are many ways to make potato salad, but if you're looking for a traditional, mayonnaise-based potato salad made with hard-boiled eggs, celery, and a little pickle relish for zing, you've landed on the right recipe. This recipe for Old-Fashioned Potato salad is a proven winner. And it's so easy to make using everyday ingredients. I'll show you how to make Old-Fashioned Potato salad, with loads of tips all along the way.

Ingredients

  • 5 medium potatoes
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup relish
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1/4 tsp garlic salt
  • 1/4 tsp celery salt
    •

    Steps

    1. Boil potatoes and eggs
    2. Let potatoes cool
    3. Chop potatoes
    4. Peel and chop eggs
    5. Mix all ingredients together in large bowl
